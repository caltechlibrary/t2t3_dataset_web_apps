Cafe con Leche

Ingredients,Units
Filtered water,fill to 8 on coffee pot
Finely-ground Cuban coffee,3 Tbsp
heated whole milk, 8 ounces


1. Prepare the coffee in drip maker by filling the coffee pot to number 8, and pour 3 Tbsp of ground Cuban express coffee into filter. Press "brew"

2. While brewing, pour the 8 ounces of milk into the electric milk frothier (max  line). Put on the lid and flip the switch to froth.

3. When coffee has finished brewing, set out two glass coffee mugs, fill from, 1/2 to 2/3 full with coffee.

4. When frothier completes, top off glass mugs with heated milk and serve.
